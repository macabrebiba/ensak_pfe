Génie Mécatronique d’automobile

La spécialité mécatronique de l’ENSA de Kénitra a pour objectif de former des ingénieurs aptes à développer des projets pluridisciplinaires intégrants de l’informatique, de l’automatisme, de l’électronique, et de la mécanique. What is ENSAK?

The National School of Applied Sciences of Kenitra (ENSAK) was created in 2008. On Monday, October 13, 2008, His Majesty King Mohammed VI laid the first stone for the construction of the premises of the establishment. ENSAK aims to train state engineers in scientific and technical fields but with skills in management and communication. The opening of ENSAK reinforces the efforts made at both national and regional level to meet the national training program for 10,000 engineers.

Pedagogical architecture?

This architecture is broadly in line with the educational reform of higher education and complies with the provisions of Law 01-00 (curriculum organized in semesters, modules, bridges, etc.).

What type of engineering i can found there?

four engineering cycles are open in specialties likely to experience significant development. These are the sectors: Computer Engineering Telecommunications networks and systems electrical engineering Industrial Engineering Automotive Mechatronics Engineering
